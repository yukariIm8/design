Solution & Impact Overview

Jun, 20, 2020

The “Life Achiever” is rooting for users to achieve their life goals. By using this app, users can see their goals anytime, can plan projects aiming to make their goals into reality, and could get inspired by their goals’ related images.

Through the iterative design process, this product has came arrived at the level that is more closely aligned with real users’ needs and goals by including feedback, testing, and validation research phase. The key features are as follows.

  1. Display Goals & Projects.
  2. Display a randomly chosen user-uploaded image.
  3. Generate suggestion for planning projects (Recommendation)

Regarding feature 3, how might we achieve higher accuracy for a recommendation? Further research, iteration, and collaboration with users will be needed.
