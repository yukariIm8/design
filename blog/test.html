Validation & Feedback

Jun, 16, 2020

To validate the design and check usability, I recruited 11 participants and conducted a usability test.

The result showed that 9 out of 11 people did not seem to understand what the "Motivational View" is, and the longest time that users spent on the "Motivational View" was 11 seconds. This is an indication that users may be confused by the "Motivational View." Changing the "Motivational View" will help the user know more about the effectiveness of this screen, so the user will not just press ok to pass, but the user will take his time to read before pressing ok and will enjoy the "Motivational View.â€œ
