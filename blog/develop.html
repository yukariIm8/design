Develop

Prototyping

Jun, 13, 2020

I first developed a style guide, which includes typography, colors, Imagery, and Icons. Then I developed a pattern library, which contains buttons, navigation, and other components (cards, dialogs, input fields, and headers) along with design do’s and don’ts.

Based on the design guides and the latest low-fidelity prototype, I designed a high-fidelity prototype. Then, I evaluated my design and examined issues and opportunities for improvement in accessibility, and finally implemented those improvements.
